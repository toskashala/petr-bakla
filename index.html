P E T R B A K L A

Born in 1980 in Prague, Petr Bakla often employs basic pitch-based material in his compositions. He is interested in constructing situations and structural contexts in which these frugal musical elements can acquire a unique expressiveness and energy. A frequent feature of Bakla's work is a simultaneous course of two musical/sound layers which are of equal importance, their "friction" creating specific tension and ambiguity.
